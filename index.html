BEING GREEN

We use soy-based inks to ensure our publishing is environmentally friendly.

The soy-based ink allows for fewer harsh chemicals to be used. The printing process is not able to use water soluble and biodegradable procedures. All of our paper products are recycled paper production.

WHO ARE WE?

Andrea and Henry Senderson

After earning his Masters degree in Mass Communications, Henry Senderson joined the Peace Corp to see the world outside Monson, Maine. It was in southern France where he met his two loves, publishing, and a young girl, his future wife Andrea. After marrying two years later, the couple returned to Maine to buy a publishing company from a family friend. The rest is history.
